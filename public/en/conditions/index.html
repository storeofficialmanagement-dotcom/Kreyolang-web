Article 1 — Purpose of the service

Kreyolang is a digital platform for learning Creole languages (Haitian Creole, Guadeloupean, Martinican, Réunionese, and other variants), developed by Christopher Jeanne EI (sole proprietor, operating under the trade name CMJ Group and the verbal trademark Kreyolang).

The platform offers a gamified learning experience inspired by the pirate universe: lessons structured by levels and thematic units, an experience-points (XP) system, league rankings, daily challenges, gem collection, and day streaks. The objective is to make Creole language learning accessible, engaging, and scientifically effective.

The service is available in a free version with limited access, and in a Premium version with full access to the entire learning content.
